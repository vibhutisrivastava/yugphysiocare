Treatment Modalities

Advanced Therapies We Offer

We combine proven modalities with the latest rehabilitation technology for faster, lasting recovery.

⚡

Interferential Therapy (IFT)

Low-frequency electrical currents target deep tissues to reduce pain, inflammation, and muscle spasms while boosting natural healing.

🔊

Ultrasound Therapy

High-frequency sound waves penetrate deeply to reduce swelling and pain, accelerating the body's cellular repair processes.

💡

LASER Therapy (LLLT)

Focused light energy stimulates cellular regeneration, reduces inflammation, and provides non-invasive pain relief.

🙌

Manual Therapy

Hands-on joint mobilisation and manipulation to restore full range of motion and relieve musculoskeletal discomfort.

💪

Therapeutic Exercise

Customised strengthening, flexibility, and balance programs that restore function and prevent future injury recurrence.

🧊

Hot & Cold Therapy

Targeted thermal treatments to reduce acute inflammation, alleviate chronic pain, and support faster muscle recovery.

📍

Dry Needling

Fine needle techniques targeting muscle trigger points to release tension, alleviate pain, and improve movement quality.

🏃

Gait & Posture Training

Expert assessment and correction of walking patterns and posture to prevent injury and optimise everyday movement.
